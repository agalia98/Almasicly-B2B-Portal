Our Story

Almasicily was born from a father's love - named after Alma, our founder's first daughter. When preparing for her weaning in 2015, we realized how difficult it was to find truly genuine, wholesome food.

This sparked the creation of a clean, transparent, and reliable company that supports local small producers, organic and sustainable agriculture principles, and slow production methods that preserve both nutritional properties and authentic taste.

We bring authentic Sicilian products to your table - products that are exactly what they claim to be and truly come from where they say they come from. That's why they're naturally better.
